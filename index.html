What I'm Working On

AfterMatters.ai

When someone passes away, the people left behind are often overwhelmed by the legal and administrative tasks that follow — estate paperwork, account closures, notifications, deadlines. It's a lot to manage during an already painful time.

AfterMatters.ai is a tool I built to help families navigate that process — organizing the steps, surfacing what needs to happen and when, and making a deeply stressful experience a little more manageable.
