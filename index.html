What I'm Working On

AfterMatters.ai

AfterMatters.ai is a step-by-step guide built to help individuals and families navigate the legal, financial, and administrative tasks that follow a death or major life transition. It is designed to bring structure, clarity, and progress tracking to a process that often feels overwhelming.

AfterMatters is currently in testing. We are also working through a developer transition, so minor temporary service disruptions may occur during this period.
