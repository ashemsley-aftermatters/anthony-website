Founder of AfterMatters.ai and Residential Real Estate Appraiser

I'm building AfterMatters.ai to help people navigate the overwhelming tasks that follow the death of a loved one or a major life transition. The platform is designed to provide structure, guidance, and progress through a process that often leaves families unsure of what to do next.

My background as a residential real estate appraiser taught me how to work through serious, document-heavy situations with care, detail, and responsibility. Today, my primary focus is building AfterMatters into a practical tool that helps families during some of life's hardest moments.

What I'm Working On

AfterMatters.ai

AfterMatters.ai is a step-by-step guide built to help individuals and families navigate the legal, financial, and administrative tasks that follow a death or major life transition. It is designed to bring structure, clarity, and progress tracking to a process that often feels overwhelming.

AfterMatters is currently in testing. We are also working through a developer transition, so minor temporary service disruptions may occur during this period.
